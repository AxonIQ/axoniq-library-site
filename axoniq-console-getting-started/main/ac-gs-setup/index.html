Introducing AxonIQ Console

The AxonIQ Console is an advanced management platform designed to maximize the effectiveness of applications developed with Axon Framework and supported through Axon Server. It facilitates near-zero configuration and provides a single platform for insight, management, control, and reporting of your application infrastructure.

Purpose and intended audience

This manual serves as a guide for engineers, developers, and system administrators who aim to leverage AxonIQ Console to oversee their message-driven systems built with AxonIQ technologies. The Console is crafted to cater to users who seek a graphical interface for an intuitive and comprehensive overview of their systems.
