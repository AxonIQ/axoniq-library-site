Using The "Settings" Tab To Manage The AxonIQ Console

Owners and Administrators have special privileges in order to manage their Workspace. This includes the capability to add and delete users, servers, and applications, and to manage access tokens.

To manage the Console as an administrator, click on the "Settings" tab.
